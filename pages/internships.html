Step by Step

How to Apply

1

Register on Internshala

Create a free student account at internshala.com using your college email and complete your profile.

2

Inform Placement Cell

Notify the NPGC Placement Cell about the internship you're applying for to get institutional support.

3

Apply & Interview

Submit your application with a tailored cover letter. Attend virtual or on-campus interview rounds.

4

Complete & Certify

Complete your internship, collect your certificate, and submit an experience report to the placement cell.
