Before You Begin

Important Instructions

1

Your password is the numeric part of your latest Fee Receipt No. e.g. Receipt 200/21912/1-A → Password: 21912

2

Verify all details carefully after login. In case of any error, contact the concerned faculty counter immediately.

3

Provide your Date of Birth, Mobile Number, and your Email ID (if available) in the form.

4

Upload your Passport Size Photograph in JPEG (less than 50 KB) and your Signature in JPEG (less than 20 KB).

5

Press the Submit button. You will receive a confirmation copy of your Examination Form on successful submission.

6

Admit card will be issued later. Students must bring both the Admit Card and i-Card to appear in the examination.

7

If a student does not have their i-Card, they must get the Admit Card verified from the faculty counter in-charge before the exam.

8

Only Regular examination forms are submitted online. Back Paper / Exempted forms must be submitted offline at the faculty counters.

9

Ensure you submit before the last date: 25th March, 2026. Late or incomplete submissions will not be entertained.

10

In case of any query, mail us at support@npgc.in. Response within 1–2 working days.
